# Expedition YEG: Customer Feedback Form Plan

Use these questions to build your Google Form for post-tour feedback.

---

## Form Introduction / Description
*Copy and paste this into the top description of your Google Form:*

"Thank you for riding with Expedition YEG! As a small, locally-owned business, your feedback means the world to us. It's how we improve our routes, train our guides, and grow our little operation here in Edmonton. Taking 3 minutes to share your thoughts is the biggest compliment you can give us. Thank you for your support!"

---

## 1. Overall Experience
*   **How would you rate your overall experience with Expedition YEG?**
    *   *Type:* Linear Scale (1 - 5)
    *   *Labels:* 1: Disappointing, 5: Exceptional
*   **How likely are you to recommend us to a friend or colleague?**
    *   *Type:* Linear Scale (1 - 10)
    *   *Labels:* 1: Not at all likely, 10: Extremely likely

## 2. Tour Details
*   **Which tour did you join?**
    *   *Type:* Multiple Choice
    *   *Options:*
        *   River Valley Cruise
        *   Historic Neighborhoods Tour
        *   Custom / Private Tour
*   **Was the physical pace of the tour appropriate for you?**
    *   *Type:* Multiple Choice
    *   *Options:* Too slow, Just right, Too fast

## 3. The Guide
*   **How would you rate your guide's knowledge and storytelling?**
    *   *Type:* Linear Scale (1 - 5)
*   **How would you rate your guide's focus on safety and group coordination?**
    *   *Type:* Linear Scale (1 - 5)

## 4. Specific Feedback
*   **What was the highlight of your tour?**
    *   *Type:* Paragraph (Long answer)
*   **Is there anything we could have done to make your experience even better?**
    *   *Type:* Paragraph (Long answer)
*   **How was the booking and preparation process? (Website, emails, waiver, etc.)**
    *   *Type:* Paragraph (Long answer)

## 5. Testimonials & Marketing
*   **Can we use your feedback (anonymously) as a testimonial on our website or social media?**
    *   *Type:* Multiple Choice
    *   *Options:* Yes, No
*   **Would you like to be added to our email list for future tour announcements and discounts?**
    *   *Type:* Multiple Choice
    *   *Options:* Yes please!, No thanks

---

### Tips for Google Forms:
1.  **Settings:** Enable "Collect email addresses" if you want to follow up with them personally.
2.  **Theme:** Use your brand colors (Blue/Orange) and upload your logo as the header image.
3.  **Confirmation Message:** Set a custom message like: *"Thank you for your feedback! It helps us keep our wheels turning and our tours improving. We hope to see you on the trails again soon!"*
